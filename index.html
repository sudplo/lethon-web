Lethon Protocol•Version 0.1.0•May 2026

The network carries what it doesn't understand.

Four locks.
One fails, three remain.
You can't break all four simultaneously.

i2pd

Carries your voice. Carries your files.
Opens a tunnel. Closes it. The address disappears.

Ephemeral by default.
The tunnel existed for 5 minutes.
Nothing else knows that address ever existed.

Tor

Finds the other person without asking a server.
A shared secret becomes an address.
Your metadata vanishes into onion routing.

No central point can see your metadata.
Tor doesn't know the content. The mesh doesn't know your IP.

Waku

Moves the message through a mesh of strangers.
Each node forwards without understanding.
Ephemeral topics erase the link between messages.

No central point of observation.
No entity sees the full conversation.
The network IS the server.
No one controls it. Everyone maintains it.

Briar

Encrypts. Ratchets. Forgets the old keys.
Forward secrecy by default.

Permissions without a central server.
Owner. Admin. Member.
All enforcement is client-side.
No server decides who can speak.

Works offline.
Sync when you reconnect.
The network doesn't disappear if Tor goes down.

Plaintext. Still human.

ChaCha20-Poly1305. The key will never exist again.

03 · Topic is born

A unique address. For this message only.
No two messages share a topic.

04 · Tor tunnels

Your IP address disappears here.
Metadata about WHERE you are: hidden.

05 · Mesh carries

Strangers forward what they don't understand.
No node sees the full path.

06 · Sentinel wakes

Checks 256 topics. One match.
The receiver's device recognizes its message.

07 · Arrives. Decrypts.
